The Problem

You Don't Have a Lead Problem. You Have a Leak Problem.

Think of your business as a bucket. Leads are the water — you're already paying to fill it with ads, referrals, and word of mouth. But most roofing companies have three holes in the bottom. Not one big crack. Three specific, predictable places where jobs walk out the door before you ever get a chance to close them.

  • 📞
    Missed calls go to voicemail Homeowner calls one more roofer. That roofer answers. You lose the job before you even knew about it.
  • 📋
    Web forms sit too long A lead fills out your form at 9pm. You see it at 8am. They booked someone else at 9:15pm.
  • 📄
    Estimates go out and nobody follows up You sent a $12,000 quote three weeks ago. You meant to call. The competitor who did is doing the job right now.

What We Install

Three Core Systems.
Wired Together. Live in 14 Days.

Each system targets one of the three places roofing leads leak. Built in GoHighLevel. Handed off to you fully operational on Day 14.

SYSTEM 01
📞
Missed Call Call-Back

Every missed call gets an automatic callback within 60 seconds — before they dial your competitor.

  • Calls the lead back within 60 seconds of a missed call
  • Qualifies the lead and routes them into your pipeline automatically
  • Logs the contact and conversation in your GHL CRM
  • Sends a follow-up text if the callback isn't answered
SYSTEM 02
🌙
After-Hours Agent

An AI agent handles inbound calls and texts after hours — qualifying leads and booking jobs while you sleep.

  • Responds to after-hours calls and texts automatically
  • Collects name, address, job type, and urgency level
  • Books them on your calendar for the next available slot
  • Notifies your team the moment the business day starts
SYSTEM 03
📋
Post-Inspection Follow-Up

Automates the gap between inspection and signed contract — so no estimate goes cold.

  • Sends estimate confirmation immediately after inspection
  • Runs automated SMS + email reminder sequence if no response
  • Triggers a no-show recovery flow for missed inspections
  • No quote disappears without a documented follow-up attempt
